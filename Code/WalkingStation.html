Advantages and Disadvantages of Outdoor Running Machine

A park running machine, also known as an outdoor air walker, is a non-electric fitness device commonly installed in public parks for cardiovascular exercise. Its main advantages are low cost, no need for electricity, durability, and simple operation. It provides low-impact movement that reduces stress on the joints and encourages people to exercise outdoors.

However, it has some limitations. The resistance level is usually fixed, so users cannot easily adjust workout intensity. It does not fully simulate natural running motion, which may reduce training effectiveness. In addition, exposure to weather can cause wear and tear if maintenance is not done regularly.
