Our story and Our Purpose

Our project is about bringing new life back into the park by creating a welcoming space where people feel encouraged to spend time, connect, and enjoy the outdoors in ways that feel natural and enjoyable. Rather than focusing on fitness, we aim to support wellbeing through community, movement, relaxation, and shared experiences, helping the park become a vibrant part of everyday life again.
